About Me

I am a hobbiest who loves all things crafts! I am attending Gonzaga University to get a Business Degree with the hopes of one day opening my own business. Currently I have my own online store where I sell crocheted clothing, amigurumi, accessories, and much more. I am also a volunteer for The Loose Ends Project, where I complete projects for people/loved ones who have either passed away or are too sick to finish their own projects.
